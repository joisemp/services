Maintainer Assignment Information
Space-Specific Maintainers

Space-specific maintainers can only be assigned to issues within their assigned spaces. They can:

  • Work on issues in assigned spaces only
  • See only issues from their assigned spaces in their dashboard
  • Focus on specialized areas or departments
  • Provide dedicated support for specific spaces
Organization-Wide Maintainers

Organization-wide maintainers can work on issues from any space in the organization. They can:

  • Be assigned to issues in any space
  • See issues from all spaces in their dashboard
  • Provide general support across the organization
  • Handle overflow when space-specific maintainers are unavailable
Assignment Priority

When assigning issues, the system will show both space-specific maintainers and organization-wide maintainers. Space-specific maintainers are recommended for specialized support, while organization-wide maintainers provide flexibility for general maintenance tasks.
